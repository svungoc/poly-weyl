Semiclassical Weyl Algebras

A semiclassical Weyl algebra is a polynomial algebra with variables \((\hbar, q_1, p_1, q_2, p_2, \dots)\). The degree in the hbar variable (\(\hbar\)) is 2.

It is a Lie algebra (LieAlg) with the so-called Moyal bracket.

At first order in ħ, the Moyal bracket reduces to the Poisson bracket. The sign convention here is opposite to the formula in the wikipedia page.
